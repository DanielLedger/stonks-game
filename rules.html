The rules:

  • You start with £15,000 to invest.
  • There are eight companies to chose from. Each one has unique characteristics.
  • If a companies' stonks reach a value of £0 or less, that company is bust, and you may no longer trade those stonks.
  • At any time, you may cash out using the very large button at the base of your screen.
  • Your score is the combined sum of your cash and the value of your stonks the instant you cash out.
